# Microcontrollers & Boards

This MOC categorizes all microcontroller units, development boards, and processors.

## Boards
- [[hardware-board-adafruit-feather-m0]] — SAMD21 Cortex-M0+ board with native USB, 3.3V logic, 2.54 mm pitch Feather footprint
- [[hardware-board-arduino-mega-2560-r3]] — ATmega2560 board with 54 digital I/O, 16 ADC, 4 UARTs — too wide for standard breadboards
- [[hardware-board-arduino-nano]] — Compact ATmega328P DIP-30 board with breadboard-friendly 0.1" pitch
- [[hardware-board-arduino-uno-r3]] — Canonical ATmega328P board, 14 digital / 6 ADC pins, shield reference footprint
- [[hardware-board-nodemcu-esp32s]] — ESP32-WROOM dev board with WiFi/BT and 38-pin breadboard-marginal footprint
- [[hardware-board-rpi-pico]] — RP2040 dual-core Cortex-M0+ with DIP form factor and castellated pads
- [[hardware-board-sparkfun-thing-plus-esp32]] — ESP32 WROOM Feather-compatible board with LiPo charging
- [[hardware-board-stm32-nucleo-64]] — STM32F401RE Cortex-M4 with Arduino UNO + Morpho headers and on-board ST-LINK
- [[hardware-board-teensy-40]] — 600 MHz ARM Cortex-M7 board with 40 I/O in a 0.6" DIP footprint
