# The EDA market is valued at 15.89 billion dollars growing at 9 percent CAGR toward 34.71 billion by 2035

Phase 5 (Feature Innovation) frames the strategic context: the EDA market is $15.89B growing at 9% CAGR toward $34.71B by 2035. Browser-based EDA is proven viable (Flux.ai, EasyEDA have paying customers). AI-assisted circuit design is entering its second wave. This means ProtoPulse is not entering a speculative market — it is entering a large, validated, growing market where the question is execution, not demand.

The maker/education/hobbyist segment that ProtoPulse targets is a fraction of this total, but even a fraction of a $35B market is substantial. More importantly, the architecture-first + AI-native approach has no direct incumbent, which means early capture of this niche is not competing for existing customers but creating new ones.
