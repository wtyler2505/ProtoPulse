# Self-hosted and free is a high-durability pricing moat against Flux.ai

Flux.ai charges $20-$158/month per editor. ProtoPulse is self-hosted and free. For the target audience — hobbyists, students, indie makers, educational institutions — this price difference is not a preference, it is a hard constraint. A high school electronics class cannot pay $158/month per seat. A weekend Arduino builder will not subscribe to design one board.

The moat durability is rated "High" in the competitive analysis because it compounds: every free user who builds a project on ProtoPulse creates switching costs. Combined with the breadboard view (which only Fritzing otherwise offers) and AI guidance, the combination of breadboard + AI + free creates a compelling bundle that no competitor currently matches at any price point.
