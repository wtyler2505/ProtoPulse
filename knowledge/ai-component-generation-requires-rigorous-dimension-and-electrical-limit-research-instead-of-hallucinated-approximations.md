# AI component generation requires rigorous dimension and electrical limit research instead of hallucinated approximations

When utilizing LLMs to generate virtual electronic components or hardware specifications, strict guardrails must be enforced. AI component generation requires rigorous dimension and electrical limit research. Relying on hallucinated approximations for pad spacing, voltage tolerances, or pin configurations leads to invalid footprints and dangerous electrical assumptions. Methodological verification against real datasheets is mandatory for EDA tool creation.
