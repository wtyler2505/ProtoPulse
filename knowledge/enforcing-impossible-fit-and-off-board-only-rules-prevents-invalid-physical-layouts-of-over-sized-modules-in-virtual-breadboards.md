# Enforcing impossible-fit and off-board-only rules prevents invalid physical layouts of over-sized modules in virtual breadboards

Virtual breadboards must enforce physical constraints to remain useful. Enforcing impossible-fit and off-board-only rules prevents invalid physical layouts, specifically for over-sized modules that cannot mechanically plug into standard 0.1-inch header spacing. Physical layout validation prevents unrealistic virtual assemblies that would mechanically fail in the real world.
