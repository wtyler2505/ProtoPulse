Iteration log format guide — pp-backlog

Purpose

Time-series, append-only record of BL-XXXX decisions, Wave history, and

 

'we tried X, didn't work, chose Y because Z' entries.

Entry shape

Title:

 

<ISO-date> — <decision-tag> (BL-XXXX if applicable)

Body:

 four fields:

Tried:

 what was attempted

Why not:

 why it didn't fit

Chose:

 what was chosen instead

Why:

 the reasoning that made it the right call

Entries are added via

/pp-iter <decision>

 slash command, or 

nlm note create pp-backlog ...

 manually.

 

Wave snapshots are appended via the weekly cron (

pp-nlm-weekly-cron.sh

).
