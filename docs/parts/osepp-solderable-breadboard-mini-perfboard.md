# OSEPP Solderable Breadboard Mini — Perfboard

A small solderable prototyping board with through-hole pads on 0.1" spacing. Use it to make permanent versions of breadboard circuits — solder your components down, wire the connections underneath, and you have a durable circuit that won't fall apart when bumped. The mini size is good for small sub-circuits: sensor breakouts, voltage regulators, adapter boards.
